Welcome to The Hungry बुद्ध Restaurant


What The Hungry बुद्ध prides itself in the most is the food- that is grown, cooked and finally brought to your table. Unlike any other restaurants, we grow food in our very own farms as we have increasingly become very wary of the poisonous foods being produced and imported to Nepal. Our owners believe that food production methods in Nepal have reached inconsumable levels with the amount of chemicals used to inject in vegetables, fruits and fertilize the soil. We grow 100% organic food using only organic resources and fertilizers without any form of chemically induced growth. Come, have a taste and decide for yourself !

About Us

A well-established and reputed restaurant located in the heart of Kathmandu city. It has several private seating areas with total capacity of 150 people. We offer you varieties of Nepali, Newari, Indian and continental cuisine with traditional music and classical dancing. The restaurant ensures the perfect dining experience with an array of cuisine option. The restaurant is open daily all year around from 11:00 am till 10:00 pm. The restaurant provides a wide range of menu based with Nepali, Newari, Indian, chinese & continental cuisine.
