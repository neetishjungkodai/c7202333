About Us

A brief history of about us

The बुद्ध restaurant is a well-established and reputed restaurant located in the heart of Kathmandu city. It has dining hall with several private seating areas with total capacity of 250 people. We offer you varieties of Nepali, Newari, Indian and continental cuisine with traditional music and classical dancing. The restaurant ensures the perfect dining experience with an array of cuisine option. The restaurant is open daily all year around from 11:00am till 10:00pm. The restaurant regularly holds mega functions like seminar, meeting, wedding, initiation ceremony, beauty contest etc. Our restaurant provides a wide range of menu based on demand that could include "a la carte" or fixed menu with Nepali, Newari, Indian, chinese & continental cuisine.
